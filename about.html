About us

Firhouse Carmel Football Club was founded in 1974 to cater for Schoolboys & girls in the local area.

The object of the Club is:

  • To foster the game of Assocuation football among all children
  • To promote a social spirit
  • To provide the best facillities possible towards achieving those aim.

We currently field team from Under 6 in our Academy right up to Under 18 in both the South Dublin Football League as well as the Dublin and District Schoolboy League. Almost 300 members wear the yellow and blue of Firhouse Carmel each week.

Based at Firhouse Community and Lesuire Centre, we have a purpose build changing and meeting facility complete with coffee shop, 4 full size football pitches and 3 7 and 9 aside pitches.

Supporting the Managers and Coaches each week, we have an active committee that looks after administration, pitch and building maintanence, coaching and fund raising.

We are delighted to be proudly sponsored by Regans Supervalu, Firhouse, whose generous support over the years has contributed greatly to our aims.
